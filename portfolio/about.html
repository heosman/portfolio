About Me

I am a student who is about to graduate from the Web Development Program at Seattle Central College. I have 2 years of experience with web development and am still exploring. I have been working towards gaining fluency in programming languages, such as HTML, CSS, PHP, JavaScript, and Python, in order to build functional and attractive websites and applications.

Front-end development has always been one of the more interesting parts of the IT field for me because as an artist, I'm immediately drawn to the visual aspect of web development and just being able to bring ideas to life in the form of a website. It's satisfying to create a website that not only looks good but functions correctly, so the user is provided with an aesthetically pleasing and interactive experience.

In my free time, I enjoy reading, painting, and watching anime, movies, and TV shows.
